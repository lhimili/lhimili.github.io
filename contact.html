OUR LOCATION

A ten minute drive from the airport; the Intare Conference Arena is located close to the special economic (free trade) zone. Located in a lushly landscaped green space, ringed by palm trees, rolling hills, vast gardens and a great horizon view, one would never know it is located right across the Kigali International Airport. Without the congestion of a downtown area, this provides ease of access for travelers and out-of-town guests. It includes extensive, free parking for over 560 vehicles in the lush gardens and an extra 400 provisional exterior parking. Guests can drive right up to the building and park.
